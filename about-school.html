Head of Institution's Welcome

Welcome to Government Primary School Pethgam Wagoora. We are committed to providing a high-quality, inclusive education to every child in our community. Our school serves as a foundation where children from Pethgam and surrounding areas begin their journey toward a bright future.

We focus on the Foundational Literacy and Numeracy (FLN) of our students, ensuring they build strong roots in a nurturing and safe environment. At our school, we don't just teach subjects; we build character, promote hygiene, and encourage the holistic development of every young learner.

- Headmaster/In-charge

Our Mission

To ensure universal access to quality primary education and provide a stimulating learning environment where every child feels valued and capable.

Our Vision

To transform our village youth into literate, responsible, and confident citizens who contribute positively to society and the nation.

Our Core Commitments

Quality Education

Focusing on the latest pedagogical techniques and child-centric learning to ensure basic concepts are clear.

Health & Nutrition

Providing hygienic Mid-Day Meals (MDM) to ensure our students remain healthy and focused on their studies.

Modern Exposure

Integrating basic digital awareness and ICT initiatives to bridge the gap between rural and urban education.
